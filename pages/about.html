About Me

To explain a bit more about myself, I am a mechatronics engineering student at the University of Waterloo, although I am originally from Vancouver, BC. I have always enjoyed solving problems and the effort that goes into that process, as I much enjoy the satisfaction and the sense of accomplishment following hard work.

My passion for the field of engineering stems from the simple desire to see actual, measureable results of my efforts. My father, who is also in the discipline, used to drive me around the city, pointing out all the projects he'd worked on, imbuing him (and in turn, me) with a sense of pride. I knew that I wanted that pride for myself one day, and decided to follow him into the field to hopefully one day attain the feeling for myself. More recently, this passion has extended to the field of software engineering and design, where I hope to solve problems and create solutions for the digital world today.
