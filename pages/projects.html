Projects and Experience

This is a list of my past and current projects, as well as some of the experiences I've had. Some of these projects are complete, while others are still in progress.

Pog da Dog

Pog da Dog is an interactive dogwalking simulator built in Unreal Engine 4. The core gameplay loop consists of walking your dog, as well as the activities and interactions related to that. The game will feature various locations and minigames based on dog-walking, as well as various breeds of dogs to walk.
