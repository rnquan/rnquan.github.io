Recent Projects

MCMC Energy Model

As part of the research group Greener Productions, I helped devlop an energy model in VBA and Python simulating the daily habits of residents in a remote Ontario community. This model used conditional probabilities in a Markov Chain Monte Carlo model, simulating each resident's hourly activities based on current hour and previous activity. This model, when compared to data from Ontario's IESO, predicted loads within 5% of measured values.

Cancer Model

Working for the Shenzhen Lansi Insitute of AI in Medicine, I helped to both develop and test a predictive model for determining the gene signature of TCGA-LIHC, or hepatocellular carcinoma, the primary liver cancer. This model used filtering algorithms in Perl and R such as multi/uni-COX regression and LASSO regression to compile a list of genes related to LIHC, intended for prognostic usage. The model, evaluated using an ROC curve, demonstrated an AUC of 0.805.

Monkey Fish

Monkey Fish is a 2.5D game based around fishing up monkeys following a flood. The game, developed in C# with Unity, features original pixel-art characters and environments, with plans for up to 20 different "fish" to reel in.
